# Welcome to ServiceNow Micro-Certification

## Course Overview

The **Welcome to ServiceNow Micro-Certification** validates the knowledge gained from the Welcome to ServiceNow course.

It includes practical simulator tasks and assessments designed to evaluate a learner's understanding of the ServiceNow platform and its core navigation concepts.

This credential acts as the first milestone in the ServiceNow learning journey before progressing to administration-focused training.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Demonstrate platform navigation skills.
- Complete simulator-based exercises.
- Apply concepts learned in Welcome to ServiceNow.
- Validate foundational platform knowledge.

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Navigation
- Lists
- Forms
- Personalizing the instance
- Basic platform usage
- Working with records
